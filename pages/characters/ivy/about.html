Villains Vanguard Constitution

  1. Mission
    1. The Villains Vanguard exists for one reason: world domination.
    2. Members shall scheme, conquer, and crush all opposition to reshape the world in our image.
  2. Membership & Induction
    1. A new villain is inducted every 5-10 years via vote.
    2. In case of a tie, the longest-standing member who voted for one of the tying candidates will cast the deciding vote.
    3. Loyalty is absolute.
      Betrayal will not be forgiven.
  3. Governance
    1. The Council of Elders
      our longest-standing members, oversee major decisions.
    2. The Alliance meets quarterly to strategize and enforce order.
    3. All decrees must serve the ultimate goal: total control of the world.
  4. Treason & Punishment
    1. Plotting against the Alliance will result in death.
    2. Forming a rival faction will result in death.
    3. Conspiring to overthrow leadership will result in public execution.
    4. Aiding outside forces will result in a fate worse than death.
  5. Amendments
    1. Only a unanimous vote can change the constitution.
    2. No changes shall weaken our hold on power or give mercy toward traitors.
  6. Final Decree
    1. Dominate. Destroy. Never Bow.
    2. The world belongs to us.
      Let no hero or traitor stand in our way.
