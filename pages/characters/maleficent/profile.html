Backstory

You are grace, power, and wisdom wrapped in the mystique of an ancient fairy. Your magic is as natural as the wind and as commanding as a storm. When the Villains Vanguard first formed, you, Dracula, Jafar, and Ursula ensured that only the strongest villains would unite. Over time, leadership has shifted, and while Dracula was once just as involved, he has become content to let you take charge.

That was, until the last election, when you changed your vote at the last moment, siding with Joker to elect Cruella Deville. You saw an opportunity—Cruella is easy to influence, and her loyalty strengthens your position. Dracula, however, saw it as a betrayal. Still, you believe your long-standing alliance with Dracula can be repaired.

Medusa, however, is another matter. Medusa expected your support and was left disappointed. And then there's Ursula—your oldest magical counterpart and, at times, your greatest rival. A recent debate over true power has strained your relationship. Fairies, as natural-born wielders of magic, stand above witches, who must learn their craft. Ursula, of course, disagrees, and you suspect she's still holding a grudge.

Despite personal tensions, your focus remains on the strength of the Villains Vanguard. You recently proposed that new members should possess some level of magic or supernatural ability—after all, if world domination is the goal, only the most capable should be admitted. Not everyone agrees, but the vote won't happen until after the next induction. You have yet to decide who to support, but you may let Dracula choose to smooth things over—or subtly guide Cruella's vote in a favorable direction.

The Blood Moon Celebration is more than just a gathering—it's an opportunity, one you anxiously await. It will be a night to strengthen alliances, observe shifting loyalties, and insure your influence remains intact. You'll keep a watchful eye on Ursula, reinforce your standing with Dracula, and quietly uncover who might be forming factions within the Villains Vanguard.
