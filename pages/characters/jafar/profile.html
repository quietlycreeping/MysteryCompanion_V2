Backstory

You are a master of charm and ambition, a visionary in velvet and silk, always playing the long game. Once a royal vizier, you built your influence in the shadows—whispering in the ears of rulers, bending laws, and shaping empires. Sorcery became your tool, deception your craft. You assumed your place in the Villains Vanguard was unshakable, but as new faces climbed the ranks, you realize reputation alone isn't enough. If you want to stay at the top, you must work for it.

For years, you let Maleficent and Dracula lead, confident they would make the right choices while you enjoyed the benefits. But as the power shifts, you've decided it's time to step up. Taking the role of the Villains Vanguard's secretary was a smart move—now, nothing happens without your knowledge. And what you know is that tensions are rising.

Maleficent's push for control has ruffled feathers, and Dracula seems to be drifting away from Maleficent's influence ever since Maleficent blindsided him in the last vote. The two had agreed on a candidate, but at the last moment, Maleficent switched sides and voted for Cruella Deville. Now, Maleficent seems to be using her vote as a means to manipulate Cruella into doing her bidding. Ursula has also distanced herself from Maleficent, though how deep that rift runs is unclear. As the only witch on the Villains Vanguard, perhaps Ursula thinks they have the unwavering support of all of the other witches – something you intend to test.

With all the rising tension within the Villains Vanguard, you believe the time has come to make your move. In the next election, you plan to vote for Queen of Hearts – someone so caught up in their tyrannical ways they won't see they're playing right into your hands.

The Blood Moon Celebration is approaching and you can hardly wait. The event is your chance to observe, charm, and position yourself exactly where you need to be. Let others rush into alliances—you will play it slow, weaving your influence where it matters most. The game is shifting, and this time, you intend to win.
